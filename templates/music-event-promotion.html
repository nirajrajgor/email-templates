About Coldplay

Coldplay is a British rock band formed in London in 1997. They achieved worldwide fame with their hit single "Yellow" in 2000. Known for their anthemic sound and visually stunning live performances, Coldplay has sold over 100 million albums worldwide, making them one of the best-selling music artists of all time. Their music often explores themes of love, hope, and unity, resonating with millions across the globe.
